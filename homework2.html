Peking duck is a famous food in Beijing. It is famous at home and abroad for its red color, tender meat, mellow taste, and fat but not greasy.

You may not have been to China, you have not been to Beijing, but please do try the Peking duck.

Generally, Peking duck weighs about 2 kilograms. It is made into willow leaves with a blade, and 108 pieces are qualified. Get on the table while it's hot and perform in front of the guests. The knife is as fast as lightning, and the pieces are skinned, which is breathtaking.

Peking duck is a famous Beijing specialty with world reputation. The beauty of the Peking duck is derived from the precious breed of Peking duck, which is the best meat duck in the world today.

Peking duck can be eaten in a variety of ways, and it is best served in lotus leaf cakes or sesame seed cakes, with appropriate condiments, such as onion segments, sweet sauce, garlic paste, etc. If you like sweet food, you can eat it with white sugar, or you can eat it with cucumber strips and green radish strips according to different seasons.

Kissinger, Nixon and Chaplin have all tasted Peking duck. CNNGo, a travel website owned by CNN, selected the 50 most delicious foods in the world, and Peking Duck ranked fifth, becoming the highest-ranked Chinese food.
